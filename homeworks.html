    Collaboration Policy

    • Homeworks must be done individually and without discussing with fellow students. There will be ample opportunities to seek help from the course staff.
    • Each submission must clearly state any assistance that you have given or received. This includes any assistance you received from AI systems like chatGPT. Such assistance is unavoidable -- and even encouraged -- in today's world but students must consult with the course staff about the extent of such assistance in advance.
    • You should not post your solutions online (e.g. in public Github repos) even after the course has ended.
    • Plagiarism may result in a mandatory drop in the letter grade, referral to the Center for Community Standards and Accountability (CSA), or a failing grade.
